ABOUT SVNIT & IGNIS

The college campus spread over 265 acres of land on the Surat-Dumas Highway, boasts of state-of-the-art academic infrastructure, with various projects currently in development phase. The campus houses ten well-equipped hostels, including one girls’ hostel, all named after eminent Indian personalities. The institute also has two guest houses, a dispensary, a post-office, a nationalized bank branch (SBI), two ATMS and a central-cafeteria providing subsidized food. Most of the institute’s sport facilities are situated in and around the Sports Activity Centre, or SAC. The SAC houses two indoor Badminton courts, a Gymnasium and Table-tennis facilities. In the vicinity of the SAC building are two basketball courts, a Lawn Tennis Court, a cricket ground, a football ground and a handball court. Other on-campus sport facilities include two volleyball courts besides the various indoor sport facilities available in hostels.

IGNIS 2016, in its sixth edition, is SVNIT’s Annual Sports Meet and is one of the biggest in Western India. SVNIT, Surat in the past has hosted several other editions of Inter-NIT Tournaments besides the five Annual meets. Filled with the elements of joy, entertainment, celebrations and fun, IGNIS is indeed one of the biggest events of its kind. The event promises to be bigger than ever this time around with several informal events being added to the mix.
